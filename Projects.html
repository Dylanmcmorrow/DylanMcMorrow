My Projects

Some videos of projects I have created in computer science classes throughout high school on a variety of platoforms.

Rockin' Robots

Created on Greenfoot

The entire game was coded in the Java programming language on Greenfoot. The objective of the game is to move the spaceship up and down using the arrow keys (horizontal motion is prohibited). Players press the spacebar to shoot, and the goal is to hit the robots and avoid the balloons. Players win by hitting all of the spaceships, all while hitting less than 2 balloons. I created this game at the end of my sophomore year of high school, in the spring of 2019.

Flamin' Fireballs

Created on Microsoft Visual Studio

First large project I've ever created. Used keyboard keys to move the avatar around the screen. The goal of the game was to move the avatar around to avoid the fireballs and collide with the ice balls to score more points. The longer a player remained "alive," the more points they scored per second, depending on the level of difficulty. The entire games was coded in the Visual Basic language. I created this game at the end of the first semester of my sophomore year of high school, during the winter of 2019.
