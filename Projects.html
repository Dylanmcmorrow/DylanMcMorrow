Flamin' Fireballs

Created on Microsoft Visual Studio

First large project I've ever created. Used keyboard keys to move the avatar around the screen. The goal of the game was to move the avatar around to avoid the fireballs and collide with the ice balls to score more points. The longer a player remained "alive," the more points they scored per second, depending on the level of difficulty. The entire games was coded in the Visual Basic language.
